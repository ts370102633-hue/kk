# Code Of Conduct

This project exists to support safe, authorized voice workflows. Contributors and maintainers are expected to keep discussions respectful, practical, and focused on improving the software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful when discussing bugs, design tradeoffs, and security concerns.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on actionable project work.
- Do not post API keys, cookies, private media links, generated files, or personal information.
- Respect speaker consent and media rights when sharing examples.
- Disclose provider cost, quota, and policy implications when proposing integrations.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Sharing private credentials, cookies, or media without permission
- Requests or contributions that enable unauthorized voice cloning, impersonation, fraud, DRM bypass, paywall bypass, or hidden scraping
- Attempts to hide provider usage, cost, or rate-limit behavior from operators

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or delete issues and pull requests that expose secrets, unsafe media, or abuse-enabling instructions.

Repeated or severe violations may lead to blocked participation in the repository.

## Reporting

For security-sensitive reports, follow [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md). Do not open a public issue containing exploit details, credentials, or private media.
